Robbie’s GYM provide members with a wide range of facilities and services, delivering an overall health and wellness experience which offers much more than just a first-rate gym.

ABOUT US

Robbie’sGYM provide the perfect destinations to stay fit and healthy as a family. Facilities include state-of-the-art gyms, heated indoor and outdoor pools, top-class racquets facilities, well-equipped group exercise studios and luxurious spas. And if you need a place to meet with friends or enjoy a bite to eat, our Gyms also feature large and inviting spaces to work, relax and socialise.

We’re passionate about making our clubs a comfortable and welcoming place to come together with friends, family and fellow members to maintain physical and mental health and wellness.

OUR QUALITY

Across our clubs, you can enjoy:

  • SWiMMING - heated indoor and outdoor pools, and lessons provided by fully qualified instructors.
  • Fitness classes - we offer a diverse range of group exercise classes.
  • State-of-the-art gyms & expert Personal Trainers
  • Racquets - match quality tennis and racquets facilities, and expert tennis coaching
  • Clubrooms - spacious areas to eat, drink and socialise
  • Kids activities & crèche - a safe and active environment for family
